AoE 2 DE (update 35584)
27 Feb 2020

Balance Changes
Berbers
Genitour receives +1 pierce armor.

Goths
Infantry are now 35% cheaper in all Ages. This bonus previously applied following the Feudal Age.

Khmer
Unique farming bonus is now disabled in Full Tech Tree games.

Mongols
Hunter work speed bonus reduced from 50% to 40%. Have access to the Steppe Lancer and Elite Steppe Lancer units. All Steppe Lancers also benefit from the +30% HP bonus.

Persians
Increased the cost of researching the Kamandaran technology from 200 food, 200 gold to 400 food, 300 gold.

Portuguese
Resources generated by Feitoria increased from 0.8 Food, 0.8 Wood, 0.45 Gold, 0.25 Stone per second to 1.6 Food, 1.0 Wood, 0.7 Gold, 0.3 Stone per second.

Tatars
Have access to Halberdier.

Teutons
Farms are 40% cheaper instead of 33%. Barracks units gain +1 armor starting from Castle Age.

Vietnamese
Economic upgrades no longer cost wood.
